How the Quiz Works

It's simple: you answer 3-4 questions about what's going on in your life and how you like to deal with things. Based on your answers, the quiz guides you to one of the 16 therapies. It takes about 2 minutes, and you'll probably laugh at least twice.

The quiz is based on the book Choose Your Own Self-Help by Luna Bolt — an interactive humor book where every path leads to a different therapy, complete with a full explainer, a humor section, and a science verdict.
